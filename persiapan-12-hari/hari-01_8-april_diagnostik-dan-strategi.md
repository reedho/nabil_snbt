## PAGI (07:00 - 09:00) — Strategi IRT & Diagnostik

### Sistem IRT dalam 5 Poin (15 menit)

1. **Jawaban salah = 0 poin** (bukan minus). JANGAN PERNAH kosongkan jawaban.
2. **Soal sulit benar = poin jauh lebih tinggi** dari soal mudah benar.
3. **Konsistensi dihargai** — salah di soal mudah tapi benar di soal sulit = dicurigai menebak.
4. **Pola jawaban dianalisis**, bukan hanya jumlah benar.
5. Bukan siapa yang paling banyak benar, tapi siapa yang **paling konsisten di level kemampuan tinggi**.

### Teknik Two-Pass (10 menit)

```
PASS 1 (60% waktu): Kerjakan soal yang bisa dijawab < 45 detik. SKIP yang berat.
PASS 2 (40% waktu): Kembali ke soal berat. Eliminasi → pilih terbaik.
SISA 5 MENIT: Isi SEMUA yang masih kosong.
```

## MALAM (18:30 - 20:00) — Kosakata Inggris Set 1 + Passage

### 15 Kata Akademik Hari Ini (20 menit)

| Kata | Arti | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| **significant** | penting, bermakna | a *significant* increase in temperature |
| **consequently** | akibatnya | Rainfall decreased; *consequently*, crops failed. |
| **despite** | meskipun | *Despite* the rain, the event continued. |
| **moreover** | selain itu | The plan is efficient. *Moreover*, it is cost-effective. |
| **nevertheless** | namun demikian | Evidence was weak. *Nevertheless*, the jury convicted. |
| **implications** | implikasi, dampak | The *implications* of climate change are severe. |
| **predominantly** | terutama | The region is *predominantly* agricultural. |
| **whereas** | sedangkan | Urban areas grow, *whereas* rural areas shrink. |
| **fundamental** | mendasar | Education is a *fundamental* right. |
| **substantial** | cukup besar | a *substantial* improvement in scores |
| **emphasis** | penekanan | placed *emphasis* on reading skills |
| **subsequent** | berikutnya | the initial report and *subsequent* studies |
| **adequate** | memadai | lacks *adequate* funding |
| **contribute** | berkontribusi | Several factors *contribute* to global warming. |
| **enhance** | meningkatkan | Technology can *enhance* learning. |

**Cara hafalkan:** Baca → tutup → coba ingat → cek. Yang lupa, tulis 3x.

### Passage + 5 Soal (30 menit)

> **The Impact of Urbanization on Mental Health**
>
> Urbanization has been one of the most significant global trends of the past century. More than half of the world's population now lives in cities, and this proportion is expected to reach 68% by 2050. While urban living offers numerous advantages, including better access to healthcare, education, and employment, researchers have increasingly turned their attention to the psychological costs of city life.
>
> Studies have consistently shown that urban residents face higher rates of anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders compared to rural counterparts. A landmark study in Nature found that city dwellers had a 21% greater risk of anxiety disorders and a 39% greater risk of mood disorders. The reasons are multifaceted: noise pollution, overcrowding, social isolation despite physical proximity, and constant sensory stimulation all contribute to mental strain.
>
> However, the relationship is not straightforward. Some cities have implemented innovative solutions, such as increasing green spaces, improving public transportation, and establishing community mental health programs. Singapore, for instance, has integrated nature into its urban planning through its "City in a Garden" initiative, which has been associated with improved well-being.
>
> The challenge for policymakers is to harness the benefits of urbanization while mitigating its psychological costs. This requires a holistic approach that considers mental health as a fundamental component of urban planning.

**1. Main idea of the passage?**
- (A) Cities are harmful to health
- (B) Urbanization brings both benefits and mental health challenges that need addressing
- (C) Rural living is better
- (D) Singapore has the best urban planning

**2. City dwellers have ___% greater risk of mood disorders:**
- (A) 21%  (B) 39%  (C) 50%  (D) 68%

**3. "Multifaceted" means:**
- (A) simple  (B) having many aspects  (C) dangerous  (D) unknown

**4. It can be inferred that:**
- (A) All cities cause equal mental health problems
- (B) Urban planning can help reduce psychological costs
- (C) People should move to rural areas
- (D) Green spaces have no effect

**5. Author's tone?**
- (A) Alarming  (B) Balanced and analytical  (C) Enthusiastic  (D) Indifferent
