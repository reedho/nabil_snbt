## PAGI (07:00 - 09:00) — Penalaran Umum: Logika

### 4 Aturan Logika — Wajib Hafal (10 menit)

| Aturan | Contoh | Valid? |
|---|---|---|
| **Modus Ponens** | Hujan→basah. Hujan. ∴ Basah | ✓ |
| **Modus Tollens** | Hujan→basah. Tidak basah. ∴ Tidak hujan | ✓ |
| **Affirming Consequent** | Hujan→basah. Basah. ∴ Hujan | ✗ SALAH |
| **Denying Antecedent** | Hujan→basah. Tidak hujan. ∴ Tidak basah | ✗ SALAH |

**Kontraposisi:** Jika P→Q, maka ¬Q→¬P (SELALU benar)

### Latihan Silogisme (20 menit)

**1.** Semua mahasiswa Teknik belajar kalkulus. Rudi belajar kalkulus. Maka:
- (A) Rudi mahasiswa Teknik (B) Rudi mungkin Teknik, mungkin tidak (C) Rudi bukan Teknik

**2.** Tidak ada siswa malas yang lulus. Beberapa siswa kelas A lulus. Maka:
- (A) Semua kelas A rajin (B) Beberapa kelas A tidak malas (C) Tidak ada yang malas di kelas A

**3.** Jika hujan, jalanan basah. Jalanan tidak basah. Maka:
- (A) Mungkin hujan (B) Tidak hujan (C) Tidak bisa disimpulkan

**4.** Jika dapat A, maka lulus. Budi tidak dapat A. Maka:
- (A) Budi tidak lulus (B) Budi mungkin lulus, mungkin tidak (C) Budi pasti lulus cara lain

**5.** Jika ekonomi tumbuh, pengangguran turun. Pengangguran turun. Maka:
- (A) Ekonomi pasti tumbuh (B) Ekonomi mungkin tumbuh, mungkin faktor lain (C) Tidak bisa disimpulkan

<details>
<summary>JAWABAN</summary>

1. **(B)** — "Semua Teknik→kalkulus" ≠ "kalkulus→Teknik" (affirming consequent)
2. **(B)** — Malas→tidak lulus. Beberapa lulus→beberapa tidak malas
3. **(B)** — Modus tollens: ¬basah→¬hujan
4. **(B)** — Denying antecedent: tidak valid. Bisa lulus cara lain.
5. **(B)** — Affirming consequent: tidak valid. Bisa faktor lain.
</details>

### 15 Transition Words (15 menit)

| Kata | Fungsi | Arti |
|---|---|---|
| **however** | Kontras | namun |
| **therefore** | Sebab-akibat | oleh karena itu |
| **in contrast** | Kontras | sebaliknya |
| **similarly** | Persamaan | demikian pula |
| **as a result** | Sebab-akibat | akibatnya |
| **on the other hand** | Kontras | di sisi lain |
| **in addition** | Tambahan | selain itu |
| **for instance** | Contoh | misalnya |
| **in particular** | Spesifik | khususnya |
| **ultimately** | Kesimpulan | pada akhirnya |
| **prior to** | Waktu | sebelum |
| **meanwhile** | Waktu | sementara itu |
| **regardless** | Konsesi | tanpa memandang |
| **provided that** | Syarat | asalkan |
| **rather than** | Pilihan | daripada |

**Kenapa penting?** Di passage, transition words = petunjuk arah:
- however/but → ide BERLAWANAN
- therefore/thus → KESIMPULAN
- for example → CONTOH
- moreover → ide TAMBAHAN searah

### Passage: AI in Healthcare (30 menit)

> AI is rapidly transforming healthcare. In diagnostics, AI algorithms can detect diseases like cancer with accuracy comparable to experienced physicians.
>
> One promising application is radiology. Deep learning systems analyze thousands of images in the time a human examines one scan. A 2020 Nature study showed Google Health's AI identified breast cancer more accurately than six radiologists, reducing false positives by 5.7% and false negatives by 9.4%.
>
> However, integration is not without controversy. Critics argue AI systems reflect biased training data. A widely used US algorithm systematically underestimated Black patients' health needs because it used healthcare spending as a proxy for health needs — and historically, less money was spent on Black patients due to systemic inequalities.
>
> Furthermore, the "black box" nature of AI raises ethical questions. When AI makes a diagnostic recommendation, physicians may struggle to understand the reasoning. If AI makes an incorrect diagnosis, who bears responsibility?
>
> Despite these challenges, the potential benefits are too significant to ignore. The key lies in robust regulatory frameworks, diverse training data, and maintaining human judgment in medical decision-making.

**3. "Proxy" means:** (A) substitute (B) exact measurement (C) opposite (D) cause

**4. Inferred about the biased algorithm:** (A) Intentionally discriminatory (B) Reflected existing societal inequalities (C) Only for Black patients (D) Banned

**5. "Black box" illustrates:** (A) AI is dangerous (B) AI is dark-colored (C) Difficulty understanding AI's reasoning (D) Developers hide code
