### OPSI A: Passage Gig Economy (jika pilih B. Inggris)

> Digital platforms like Uber, Grab, and Gojek have given birth to the "gig economy" — short-term, flexible, freelance work rather than permanent employment. In developing countries like Indonesia, a motorcycle and smartphone are often all needed to begin earning.
>
> Proponents argue the gig economy empowers workers with schedule autonomy and income supplementation. For consumers: greater convenience, competitive pricing, wider services.
>
> Critics contend it merely disguises precarious employment as entrepreneurship. Gig workers typically lack health insurance, pension, minimum wage, and protection against unfair dismissal. An ILO study found Southeast Asian gig workers earned 30% less per hour than formal workers when benefits were factored in.
>
> The challenge: regulate without stifling innovation and flexibility. Some countries experiment with a "third category" of worker — neither fully employed nor independent — entitling gig workers to certain protections while preserving flexibility.

**1. "Precarious" means:** (A) stable (B) profitable (C) uncertain and risky (D) exciting

**2. Gig workers earn ___% less:** (A) 20% (B) 25% (C) 30% (D) 40%

**3. Author:** (A) Fully supports gig economy (B) Wants to ban platforms (C) Sees both benefits and problems (D) Only formal employment acceptable

**4. "Third category" aims to:** (A) Eliminate gig work (B) Balance protection with flexibility (C) More unemployment (D) Give platforms more control

<details>
<summary>JAWABAN</summary>

1. **(C)** 2. **(C) 30%** 3. **(C)** 4. **(B)**
</details>

### OPSI B: Soal PU Lanjutan (jika pilih Penalaran)

**Soal 1-3:** Premis:
- Jika Ali ke bioskop, Budi juga pergi
- Jika Citra tidak pergi, Dedi pergi
- Ali atau Citra pergi (bisa keduanya)
- Dedi tidak pergi

**1.** Siapa PASTI pergi? (A) Ali (B) Citra (C) Ali dan Budi (D) Citra dan Ali
**2.** Apakah Budi pergi? (A) Pasti (B) Pasti tidak (C) Belum tentu
**3.** PASTI benar: (A) Ali dan Dedi pergi (B) Citra pergi (C) Budi dan Citra pergi

**Pola angka:**
**4.** 2, 6, 18, 54, ... → (A) 108 (B) 162 (C) 148
**5.** 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, ... → (A) 18 (B) 20 (C) 21
**6.** 3, 5, 9, 15, 23, ... → (A) 31 (B) 33 (C) 35
**7.** 100, 95, 85, 70, 50, ... → (A) 30 (B) 25 (C) 20

**Analogi:**
**8.** PANAS:DINGIN = GELAP:... → (A) Malam (B) Terang (C) Suram
**9.** KAYU:MEJA = BENANG:... → (A) Jarum (B) Kain (C) Jahit
**10.** 4:16 = 7:... → (A) 28 (B) 49 (C) 14
