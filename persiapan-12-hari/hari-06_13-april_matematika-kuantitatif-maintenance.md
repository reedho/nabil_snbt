### Shortcut Wajib Ingat (15 menit)

**Rata-rata cepat:**
```
Rata-rata 5 bilangan = 40 → total = 200
Hapus 1, rata-rata jadi 35 → total baru = 140
Bilangan yang dihapus = 200 - 140 = 60
```

**Persentase cepat:**
```
Naik 80→100 = (20/80)×100% = 25%
Turun 100→80 = (20/100)×100% = 20%
⚠️ Naik 20% lalu turun 20% ≠ kembali awal! (100→120→96 = turun 4%)
```

**Peluang:**
```
P(A dan B) = P(A) × P(B)          [independen]
P(A atau B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A∩B)
P(tidak A) = 1 - P(A)
```
