### Latihan Vocabulary in Context (30 menit)

> The transition to renewable energy is (1)**imperative** for addressing climate change. Fossil fuels have (2)**fueled** economic growth, but their use poses (3)**grave** risks. Governments are (4)**grappling** with balancing development and sustainability. Denmark generates >50% electricity from wind — a feat once considered (5)**inconceivable**. However, transition has (6)**hurdles**: the (7)**intermittent** nature of wind/solar requires storage solutions. Communities (8)**dependent** on fossil fuels face economic (9)**disruption**.

**1. "imperative":** (A) optional (B) essential (C) impossible
**2. "fueled":** (A) burned (B) destroyed (C) driven/powered
**3. "grave":** (A) burial place (B) serious (C) minor
**4. "grappling":** (A) agreeing (B) ignoring (C) struggling
**5. "inconceivable":** (A) likely (B) unimaginable (C) simple
**6. "hurdles":** (A) goals (B) benefits (C) obstacles
**7. "intermittent":** (A) constant (B) powerful (C) irregular/not continuous
**8. "disruption":** (A) improvement (B) disturbance (C) celebration

<details>
<summary>JAWABAN</summary>

1. **(B)** 2. **(C)** — metafora 3. **(B)** — bukan kuburan! 4. **(C)** 5. **(B)** 6. **(C)** 7. **(C)** 8. **(B)**
</details>
